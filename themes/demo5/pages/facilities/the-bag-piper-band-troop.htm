The Mahabodhi School Band was the first school band party of entire Ladakh. The school has a well-equipped band set with a variety of band instruments. The band troop, consisting mostly of the hostel boys and girls with special uniform, has gained public admiration for its participation on occasions like Independence Day and Republic Day. The troop has won many prizes and appreciation from the local government & organization.
